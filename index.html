What is a  School Tool

A school tool is a communication software designed for educational institutions to increase school productivity and improve communication with parents and students. A school tool comes with various school communication features. For example, it helps the school administration share school-wide announcements, monitor parent engagement, enable teachers and parents to engage in conversations, distribute class materials, conduct live broadcasting and much more.

Like any other software technology, a school tool aims to establish a secure communication system that can overcome the barriers of ineffective communication channels like SMS and Email and move towards a more transparent and privacy-oriented software solution that allows unifying all school communication activities. However, the adoption of a School app as a go-to communication software solution arose with the demands of modern parents to have uninterrupted school communication and the need for schools to provide such an environment while still monitoring and managing the entire communication activities in the software.

How does a School Tool work

A school tool connects school staff, teachers, parents, and students in a secure and private ecosystem, where communication and engagement happen under the direct supervision of the school. It is a communication system that ensures security to everyone involved and brings more opportunities and outcomes for students.

Compose

School administrators and teachers create messages and school updates to communicate with parents and students

Approve

Parents respond to messages from school and initiate conversations or respond to messages from teachers and school administrators.

Analyze

School analyzes parent engagement, gain feedback about school activities to improve parent engagement and classroom productivity

The main aim of a school tool is to empower schools with advanced communication features like Instant Chat, Live broadcasting, Stories, Cloud storage, Emergency Messaging, and more. This software also helps improve enhanced parent-teacher engagement. It facilitates a safe communication space where everyone in the school community can share ideas, opinions, exchange feedback, and create better opportunities for children.

Where is a School tool used
and by whom

A school tool is mainly adopted by educational institutions. But it is an excellent option for schools and educational institutions of any size that want to scale the communication activities and want to implement a communication infrastructure that is reliable, efficient, and ensures protection for everyone in the school community.

Schools

View, edit and send announcements, updates, and messages and reach parents and students. Manage entire communication activities, review engagement reports and create, edit or delete user accounts and files.

Teachers

Can initiate chat, sent requests, share class materials, homework, and conduct live sessions with parents and students

Parents

Initiate chat, send messages and documents, and access homework shared by teachers.

Students

Access rewards and challenges profile, reply to message requests, and upload files requested by teachers.

Many educational institutions gravitate towards this software because it enables the administration to control the entire activities happening inside it entirely. So, in other words, a school tool is often implemented by schools when they want to implement a secure and safe space to conduct all their communication channels and replace traditional channels like emails and SMSs.

Benefits of using a School tool

When it comes to dedicated school communication software, the school tool offers more school-oriented features and functionalities to create and retain a more engaging and effective school communication system than a traditional channel. Here are 6 benefits you can get by adding a school tool to your school or educational institutions:

All communication at one place

A school tool combines the features of emails and SMS in one single platform with more added school-oriented features and interactive functions. So when compared to traditional communication channels that provide school updates scattered across channels, a school tool unifies it as most of these functions are integrated into it.

Moreover, most of the features of a school tool are designed to meet the requirements and needs of a school in mind, giving it enhanced flexibility to manage and facilitate any needs or requirements the school may face during the communication process. Apart from that, all the activities and engagement can be monitored and controlled by the school authorities. In other words, a school tool enables the school administration to take full ownership of all the communication activities that happen inside the school. This way, the school can create a safe and secure school communication ecosystem for everyone in the school community.

Enhanced parent engagement

The school tools allow location independence. So when parents need to have a conversation with the teachers or the school administrators, they connect with them instantly with the built-in chat facility, no matter where they are. When parents can get involved in conversations and talk freely with teachers, they feel more part of the school and this instant engagement brings more promising opportunities for schools and children.

Parents feel they're a part of the classroom

Since school tools allow parents and teachers to interact from anywhere to discuss school activities, share feedback, and resolve issues, the school can implement the ideas and inputs of parents in the classroom, thereby providing children with a better classroom environment that brings out the potential in them. Furthermore, when parents feel their options are heard and heard by the school administration, they feel more encouraged to participate in the school activities and feel part of the classroom.

Easy to connect with students

A school tool lets teachers connect with their students quickly by making use of the built-in messaging features. Uninterrupted communication with teachers paves the way for students to be more engaged with the class effectively. The advanced live broadcasting feature in the school tool also ensures the teacher can quickly interact with students easily no matter where they are and provide a comfortable learning experience for children at home. Moreover, most advanced school tools also enable teachers to send class materials, homework, and other school documents to parents and students via the cloud. As a result, parents and teachers can connect and assist children at home to get ahead with their academics.

Highly optimized and organized school activities

As school tools ensure uninterrupted connectivity with parents and students, all the communication activities of the school are streamlined, so all the school events, parent-teacher meetings, and other school events and functions happen smoothly at the designated time without any delays, thereby enabling the school administrators to save time and be more productive.

Creates an encouraging school ecosystem

As a school tool provides a full-fledged communication system that unifies all communication activities and brings the entire school community together under one platform, it naturally paves the way for an encouraging and positive school communicating ecosystem that takes care of itself and brings out a positive culture that fosters growth.

Top features to look out for in a
School tool

The market offers a wide range of school tools to help schools improve their communication system and increase parent engagement and classroom productivity. But each school tool focuses on various purposes of communication and school productivity, so it's essential to have an in-depth look at the best school tool available in the market and shortlist the one that matches your current requirements and goals. Here are some key features you might consider before deciding to use a tool to test out the features, interfaces, and capabilities it provides, before choosing one.

Intuitive User Interface

While choosing a school tool, you must select one that provides an easy to use user-interface. A clean and straightforward user-interface design encourages taking advantage of the functionalities and features of the software. In addition, it also makes it easy for people without any technical background to operate the software without any issues. In simple words, an exemplary user interface makes it easy for everyone in the school community to use the software.

So while narrowing it down to a single school tool, you must have a picture of how a user interface must work out for you as you're about to invest your money and resources in implementing one in your school. So, when you're looking into different software, pay attention to the entire app and functionalities. When it comes to communication, platforms that have taken design cues from social media and are made in mind to handle a community network are the best platforms to choose when narrowing down to a school communication app.

The school tool should be easy to use and ensure maximum flexibility. It must have the option to communicate with parents, teachers, and students from anywhere easily. In addition to that, while choosing software, it must be considered that the school tool you select must be modern and follow a minimalistic and engaging design that has all the necessary features to overcome the limitations of traditional platforms like emails and SMS.

Instant Chat Facility

When it comes to communication, the availability of an instant chat feature enables parents and teachers to connect instantly and create conversations. This instant connectivity paves the way for parents and teachers to interact, discuss any issues children may face in the classroom, and resolve them instantly. Thereby providing a more encouraging classroom environment for children.

So while choosing a school tool, you should check whether a secure and private one-on-one chat feature is built-in, so you can message privately to parents and students swiftly and respond to messages in one place, aiding significant improvements in engagement rates and overall communication.

Emergency Messaging Feature

Emergency messaging is a feature that enables schools and teachers to reach parents swiftly in case of an emergency. While choosing a school tool, you must check if you have an inbuilt emergency messaging feature to alert parents in times of need.

Analyze Engagement Report

As the school tool unifies all communication activities, it's a must that the school administrators access parent engagement reports. The ability to analyze engagement reports helps the schools measure parent engagement, which the school administrators can use to understand parent's overall feelings on the classroom conditions, the teaching strategies implemented in the classroom, and collect feedback, so the school can work towards making good decisions.

Collaborative features

The school tool must have enough collaborative features like Stories that enable teachers to engage with parents by showcasing the activities of their children in the classroom with pictures and videos. This way, the teachers and parents can strike conversations and create rapport, which will lead to better teacher-parent relationships. Moreover, when children feel the parents are in constant touch with teachers and know about all their classroom activities, they feel more secure and act more open to teachers and perform well in the classroom.
